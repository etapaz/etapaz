Papas Fritas - Ella me pide mas

What inspired you to write Papas Fritas?
I was playing on the floor with my daughter, and said "if we invented a funny song?".
I'm Belgian, land of french fries, so the theme was quickly found.
Who doesn't enjoy a good french fry?
We started singing.

Was writing it in response to something significant that happened in your life?
My business was heavily impacted by the COVID pandemic.
I had more free time to think about and occupy myself with other things.
In this case another more benign virus. The music one came back to me very quickly.

Where were you when you wrote and recorded it?
At home, the idea really started on the ground, on the same floor, then I got up and called 2 musician contacts, the first one for the instrumental part and the second one for the lyrics and the melody. Then a third one for the mix and mastering and it was done.

What are the ideas behind -Ella me pide mas-?
To have fun above all, to have fun to entertain the public in return, to have a good time. To escape for a moment from everyday life to live a surprising adventure.

What's the connection between your instruments, bandmates, and collaborators?
To tell you the truth, my music is made possible by the magic of today's connected world, I know very little about these collaborators who live thousands of miles away from me, but I can quickly identify if we are on the same page.
